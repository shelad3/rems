Everything You Need

Designed for property managers, landlords, caretakers, and tenants.

🏠

Property & Unit Tracking

Manage unlimited properties and units. Track vacancy, rent amounts, and status in real-time with Firestore sync.

🔍

Tenant Discovery

Tenants can browse vacant units across all properties, view building details, and apply directly from their phone.

🤝

Apply & Negotiate

Full negotiation workflow. Tenants propose rent terms, caretakers counter-offer, and both sides accept or decline.

🔧

Maintenance Board

Kanban-style maintenance tracking: Open → In Progress → Resolved. Tenants report issues, caretakers assign and track.

💬

In-App Messaging

Real-time chat between tenants and caretakers per unit. Discuss terms, ask questions, resolve issues without leaving the app.

📊

Dashboard & Reports

Role-specific dashboards with occupancy stats, revenue charts, and PDF export for rent rolls and maintenance reports.

👥

Role-Based Access

Four distinct roles: Owner (read-only), Landlord (full control), Caretaker (operations), Tenant (self-service).

☁️

Cloud Sync

All data syncs to Firestore in real-time. Offline-first with SQLite local storage — works with or without internet.

Built for Every Role

Each role gets a tailored experience with the right tools and permissions.

👑

Owner

Read-only dashboard with occupancy stats, revenue tracking, and property performance reports.

💼

Landlord

Full administrative control over properties, leases, staff accounts, and system configuration.

🛠️

Caretaker

Review tenant applications, manage maintenance tickets, assign units, and communicate with tenants.

🔑

Tenant

Discover vacant units, apply with custom terms, view lease details, pay rent, and report maintenance issues.
